EDNA-1 analyzes your touch behavior, scroll patterns, typing rhythm, and device sensors in real-time.

Humans produce naturally varied timing, imperfect movements, and reading pauses. Bots don't.

16 behavioral signals are combined into a single confidence score. When 75% of signals confirm human-like behavior, the vault opens.

No name. No email. No phone number. Just behavior. That is Human Proof.

The way you scroll through this text right now is generating data. Fast scroll means you're skimming. Slow scroll means you're reading. Scroll back up means something caught your eye.

Every pause tells a story. Every correction reveals a thinking process. Every tap leaves a behavioral fingerprint.

This is not surveillance. No content is recorded. No identity is stored. Only the statistical shape of your behavior — the rhythm, the tempo, the hesitations — is analyzed.

A bot can simulate clicks. A bot can generate text. But a bot cannot simulate doubt. And doubt is the most human thing there is.
